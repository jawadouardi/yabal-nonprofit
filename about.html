Our Mission & Purpose

Y'abal is a Belgian non-profit organisation (Association Sans But Lucratif / Vereniging Zonder Winstoogmerk) founded on 6 November 2016 in Hasselt, Belgium. Our mission is to promote the social and economic advancement of disadvantaged groups in Belgium and across Europe.

We believe that every person, regardless of their background, nationality, or circumstances, deserves access to quality education, meaningful employment opportunities, and a supportive community. This belief drives everything we do.

Statutory Purpose

As stated in our statutes registered with the Belgian Crossroads Bank for Enterprises, Y'abal's primary purpose is the promotion of all forms of social and economic advancement of disadvantaged groups. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Providing education, training, and skills development programmes
  • Supporting economic integration through micro-enterprise development
  • Facilitating access to employment and professional opportunities
  • Promoting social inclusion through community engagement activities
  • Organising cultural exchange and integration programmes
  • Raising awareness about social inequality and advocating for change

Our Values

At Y'abal, our work is guided by four core values that inform every programme and decision we make:

Inclusion: We welcome people from all backgrounds and actively work to remove barriers to participation. Our programmes are designed to be accessible and culturally sensitive.

Empowerment: Rather than creating dependency, we focus on building skills and capacities that allow individuals and communities to become self-sufficient and thrive independently.

Transparency: We maintain open and honest communication with our donors, partners, and the communities we serve. Our financial records and programme outcomes are publicly available.

Collaboration: We believe in the power of partnership. By working together with local organisations, government agencies, and international partners, we amplify our impact.

Our Journey

From our founding in Hasselt to the organisation we are today, Y'abal has grown steadily while staying true to our mission.

2016

Foundation: Y'abal is established as an ASBL in Hasselt, Belgium, by a group of passionate individuals committed to social justice. Our founding statutes are registered with the Crossroads Bank for Enterprises.

2017

First Programme: We launch our initial education programme, offering literacy and language courses to newly arrived immigrants in the Limburg region. Within the first year, we serve over 50 participants.

2018

Expansion: Y'abal grows to include economic empowerment activities, providing financial literacy training and micro-enterprise support. We establish our first partnerships with local businesses in Hasselt.

2019

Community Programmes: We introduce our community development programme, organising neighbourhood events and cultural exchange activities that bring together diverse communities across Limburg.

2020-2021

Adapting to Change: During the global pandemic, we rapidly adapted our programmes to digital formats, ensuring continued support for our beneficiaries. We launched online training platforms and virtual mentoring sessions.

2022-2023

Growing Impact: Y'abal reaches over 2,000 beneficiaries across all programmes. We expand our volunteer base to 150+ active members and establish new partnerships with European social enterprises.

2024

Looking Forward: We continue to strengthen our programmes and explore new ways to serve disadvantaged communities. Our focus remains on sustainable, measurable impact that creates lasting change.
