Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Y'abal and our programmes.

We welcome volunteers from all backgrounds. To get started, fill out the contact form above selecting "Volunteering Opportunities" as the subject, or email us at ouajoa2@gmail.com. We'll invite you for an introductory meeting to discuss your skills and interests, and match you with a programme that suits you best.

Yes, all of our programmes are open to individuals who meet the eligibility criteria. Most of our programmes target disadvantaged individuals residing in Belgium. Contact us to discuss your situation and find the right programme for you. All programmes are provided free of charge.

Y'abal is funded through a combination of individual donations, grants from foundations and government programmes, corporate partnerships, and fundraising events. We are committed to transparency and publish our financial information on our Impact page.

As a registered Belgian ASBL, donations to Y'abal may be tax-deductible for Belgian taxpayers under the country's tax reduction system for donations to recognised non-profit organisations. Contact us at ouajoa2@gmail.com for specific details about tax benefits for your donation.

We offer several partnership opportunities for businesses, including corporate volunteering programmes, employee matching gift schemes, event sponsorships, and cause-related marketing partnerships. Contact us to explore how a partnership can benefit both your business and disadvantaged communities.

Our office is located at Villerspark 4/6, 3500 Hasselt, Belgium. Our programmes operate across the Limburg region and, in some cases, in other parts of Belgium. You can visit us during office hours (Monday-Friday, 9AM-5PM) or contact us by phone or email.

Y'abal

Y'abal is a Belgian non-profit organisation (ASBL) dedicated to promoting the social and economic advancement of disadvantaged groups in Belgium and across Europe. Founded in 2016 in Hasselt, we work to create lasting change through education, economic development, and community building.
