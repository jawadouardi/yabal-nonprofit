Where Your Money Goes

We believe in full transparency about how donor funds are used:

  • 82% — Direct programme delivery (education, economic empowerment, community development)
  • 12% — Programme support and coordination
  • 6% — Administration and governance

Tax Benefits

Y'abal is a registered Belgian ASBL (non-profit organisation). Depending on your jurisdiction, donations to Y'abal may be tax-deductible. Belgian taxpayers can benefit from the tax reduction system for donations to recognised non-profit organisations.

For more information about tax-deductible donations, please contact us at ouajoa2@gmail.com.

Y'abal

Y'abal is a Belgian non-profit organisation (ASBL) dedicated to promoting the social and economic advancement of disadvantaged groups in Belgium and across Europe. Founded in 2016 in Hasselt, we work to create lasting change through education, economic development, and community building.
