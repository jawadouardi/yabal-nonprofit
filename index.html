Core Program Areas

What We Do

Y'abal operates three interconnected program areas that address the most pressing challenges facing disadvantaged communities today.

📚

Education Initiative

We provide literacy programs, vocational training, language courses, and digital skills workshops that equip individuals with the knowledge and abilities needed to participate fully in society and the workforce.

💼

Economic Empowerment

Through micro-enterprise support, financial literacy training, and job placement assistance, we help individuals and families achieve financial independence and build sustainable livelihoods.

🤝

Community Development

We foster inclusive communities through neighbourhood engagement, cultural exchange programmes, youth mentorship, and partnerships with local organisations that create lasting social cohesion.

Y'abal

Y'abal is a Belgian non-profit organisation (ASBL) dedicated to promoting the social and economic advancement of disadvantaged groups in Belgium and across Europe. Founded in 2016 in Hasselt, we work to create lasting change through education, economic development, and community building.
