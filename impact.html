Programme Outcomes

Detailed results from each of our three core programme areas.

📚

Education Initiative

1,200+ individuals have participated in our education programmes since 2016.

  • 82% course completion rate
  • 75% of language programme graduates achieved B1 proficiency
  • 350+ vocational certificates awarded
  • 89% of participants reported improved employability
💼

Economic Empowerment

800+ individuals have benefited from our economic empowerment programmes.

  • 68% job placement rate within 6 months
  • 120+ micro-enterprises launched
  • 92% financial literacy course completion rate
  • Average income increase of 40% for programme graduates
🤝

Community Development

500+ community members engaged through our community development activities.

  • 24+ community events organised annually
  • 40+ active partner organisations
  • 150+ active volunteers
  • 91% of participants felt more connected to their community

Stories of Change

Real stories from the people whose lives have been transformed by Y'abal's programmes.

Amina's Journey

"When I arrived in Belgium, I couldn't speak Dutch and had no idea how to navigate the system. Y'abal's language programme gave me the skills to communicate, and the vocational training programme helped me find work as a healthcare assistant. Today, I'm employed full-time and supporting my family independently."

— Amina, Language & Vocational Programme Graduate, 2022

Kofi's Micro-Enterprise

"Y'abal didn't just give me a small grant to start my catering business — they gave me a mentor who helped me write a business plan, understand Belgian regulations, and find my first customers. Two years later, my business employs three people from my community."

— Kofi, Micro-Enterprise Programme Graduate, 2021

Y'abal

Y'abal is a Belgian non-profit organisation (ASBL) dedicated to promoting the social and economic advancement of disadvantaged groups in Belgium and across Europe. Founded in 2016 in Hasselt, we work to create lasting change through education, economic development, and community building.
